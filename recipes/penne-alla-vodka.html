Penne Alla Vodka

This is an easy-to-make recipe that delivers on flavor and comfort, ready in a litte more than 35 minutes. Features a rich creamy vodka pasta sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 cup Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 Tbsp Vodka
  • Kosher Salt
  • 1 lb Tubed Pasta
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/2 cup Finely Grated Parmesan
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped
  • 1 Shallot, Finely Chopped
  • Fresh Basil Leaves

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Add shallot and garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until softened, 4 to 5 minutes. Add tomato paste and red pepper flakes and cook, stirring frequently, until paste has coated shallots and garlic and is beginning to darken, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add vodka and stir to incorporate, scraping up any browned bits from bottom of pot. Remove from heat.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta, stirring occasionally, until al dente according to package directions. Drain, reserving 2 cups pasta water.
  4. Return sauce to medium heat and add cream and 1/4 cup pasta water, stirring to combine. Add half of Parmesan and stir until melted. Remove from heat and stir in cooked pasta. Fold in remaining Parmesan, adding more pasta water (about 1 Tbsp. at a time) if the sauce is looking dry; season with salt, if needed.
  5. Divide pasta among bowls. Top with Parmesan and basil.
